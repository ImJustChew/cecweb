Our History
Creation of our club
In January 2014, Creative Electronics Club (CEC) was founded by a group of parents interested in electronics and maker education. So far, Creative Electronics Club consists of six coaches who have background and experience in electronic engineering and creative thinking. The purpose of the CEC was to master students' understanding of electronics, to promote a creative atmosphere, to stimulate students' potential on technology, science, engineering, and mathematics, and to lay bright future for students' future development. The course is based on the students' after-school hobbies. CEC is not a formal technical training class, but CEC still focuses on the students' academics. Our teaching method is to avoid using traditional words and theories to teach. The teaching methods are mainly based on physical internships to inspire students' interest in electronic creativity.

Club's Aim
Vision：
1) Inspire students' potential for electronic technology, science, engineering, and mathematics.
2) Develop a positive interest in students.
Mission：
1) Produce the electronic gadgets required by the school.
2) Represent our school in competitions
3) Apply STEAM in daily life.
Creed:
1) Believe you can change the world.
2) Work quickly, work at any time, and the tools are on call.
3) Understand when to be independent and when to work with the team.
4) Share your ideas and tools with your teammates and trust them.
5) Do not play politics and put an end to bureaucracy.
6) User decides if your work is done well.
7) Radical ideas are not always a bad idea.
8) Create different working methods.
9) Contribute every day. If you don't contribute on the day, you are not leaving the makerspace.
10) Believe that teamwork can be done in all things.
11) Invention and innovation.
