Our History
Creation of our club
In January 2014, Creative Electronics Club (CEC) was founded by a group of parents interested in electronics and maker education. So far, Creative Electronics Club consists of six coaches who have background and experience in electronic engineering and creative thinking.
The purpose of the CEC was to master students' understanding of electronics, to promote a creative atmosphere, to stimulate students' potential on technology, science, engineering, and mathematics, and to lay bright future for students' future development. The course is based on the students' after-school hobbies. CEC is not a formal technical training class, but CEC still focuses on the students' academics. Our teaching method is to avoid using traditional words and theories to teach. The teaching methods are mainly based on physical internships to inspire students' interest in electronic creativity.


Club's Aim
Vision：
1) Inspire students' potential for electronic technology, science, engineering, and mathematics.
2) Develop a positive interest in students.
Mission：
1) Develop and produce the electronic gadgets required by the school.
2) Participating competitions representing our school and win the competition
3) Let students implement the application of electronic technology, science, engineering and mathematics in daily life.
Creed:
1) Believe that you can change the world.
2) Work quickly, work at any time, and the tools are on call.
3) Understand when to be independent and when to work with the team.
4) Share your ideas and tools with your teammates and trust them.
5) Do not play politics and put an end to bureaucracy.
6) It is up to the user to decide if your work is doing well.
7) Radical ideas are not completely a bad idea.
8) Create different working methods.
9) Make a contribution every day. If you don't contribute on the day, you will not leave the maker room.
10) Believe that teamwork can be done in all things.
11) Invention and innovation.
