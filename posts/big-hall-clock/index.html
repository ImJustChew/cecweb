Big Hall Clock
 
The school needed another clock, so why not do another one? This is the most advanced clock we have every made. It is also the biggest clock we have created.
30 precisely cut segments fitted with custom RGB LED boards to create a 7 segment display with controllable colors. The clock gets its time from the internet through WiFi, and with standards today, that time is accurate within milliseconds. With the onboard light sensors, we can automatically tune the brightness of the clock acccording to the environment.
The clock now sits on the back of our schools assembly hall, and everyone who walks in will take a peek at it.
